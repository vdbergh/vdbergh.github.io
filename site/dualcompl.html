Title

Existence theorems for dualizing complexes over non-commutative graded and filtered rings

Author(s)

M. Van den Bergh.

Year

1996

Abstract

In this note we prove existence theorems for dualizing complexes over graded and filtered rings, thereby generalizing some results by Zhang, Yekutieli and J{\o}rgensen.
