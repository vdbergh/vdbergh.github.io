Title

A class of counter examples to the Gelfand-Kirillov conjecture.

Author(s)

J. Alev, A. Ooms, M. Van den Bergh.

Year

1994

Abstract

Let G be a connected non-special semisimple algebraic group and let W be a finite dimensional G-representation such that W has trivial generic stabilizer. Let g=Lie(G). Then the semi-direct product g+W is a counter example to the Gel'fand-Kirillov conjecture.
