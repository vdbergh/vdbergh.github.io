Title

A relation between Hochschildt homology and cohomology for Gorenstein rings

Author(s)

M. Van den Bergh.

Year

1996

Abstract

Let ``$HH$'' stand for Hochschildt (co)homology. In this note we show that for many rings $A$ there exists $d\in\NN$ such that for an arbitrary $A$-bimodule $N$ we have $HH^i(N)=HH_{d-i}(N) $. Such a result may be viewed as an analog of Poincare duality. Combining this equality with a computation of Soergel allows one to compute the Hochschildt homology of a regular minimal primitive quotient of an enveloping algebra of a semisimple Lie algebra, answering a question of Polo.
