Title

Normalizing Extensions of the Veronese of a Three Dimensional Artin-Schelter Regular Algebra on Two Generators

Author(s)

K. Bauwens, M. Van den Bergh.

Year

1996

Abstract

We classify regular algebras of global dimension four that map surjectively onto the two-Veronese of a regular algebra of global dimension three on two generators. We also study the point modules.
