Algebra publication

Title

Homological properties os Sklyanin algebras.

Author(s)

M. Van den Bergh.

Year

1994

Abstract

Let G be a connected reductive algebraic group over an algebraically closed field of characteristic zero and let W, U two finite dimensional representations of G. In this paper we compute the local cohomology of (U tensor SW)^G provided a certain relatively weak technical condition is true.
