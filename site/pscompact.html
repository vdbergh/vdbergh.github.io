Title

Existence theorems for dualizing complexes over non-commutative graded and filtered rings

Author(s)

M. Van Gastel. M. Van den Bergh.

Year

1996

Abstract

Let $A$ be a three dimensional Artin-Schelter regular algebra. We give a description of the category of finitely generated $A$-modules of Gelfand-Kirillov dimension one (modulo those of finite dimension over the ground field). The proof is based upon a result by Gabriel which says that locally finite categories can be described by module categories over topological rings.
