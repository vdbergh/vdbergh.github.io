Title

Local cohomology of modules of covariants.

Author(s)

M. Van den Bergh.

Year

1994

Abstract

Let G be a connected reductive algebraic group over an algebraically closed field of characteristic zero and let W, U two finite dimensional representations of G. In this paper we give a survey on the computation of the local cohomology of (U tensor SW)^G.
